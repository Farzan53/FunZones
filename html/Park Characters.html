Welcome to Park Characters
Discover the Enchanting Characters that bring FunZone Park to life. Each one tells a Magical Story!

Meet FunZone Characters

Mickey Mouse

The cheerful leader of Disneyland! He once sailed a steamboat and now rules our hearts.

Minnie Mouse

Sweet, classy, and always in style. Minnie spreads joy wherever she goes.

Donald Duck

A bit grumpy but always loyal! Donald’s wild adventures make us laugh every time.

Goofy

Clumsy, kind, and always trying! Goofy reminds us to laugh at life’s little goofs.

Pluto

Mickey’s faithful dog, always wagging and always loyal. A best friend without words.

Elsa

Queen of ice and love. Elsa teaches us to “let it go” and be who we truly are.

Anna

Brave, caring, and filled with laughter. Anna’s warm heart saves Arendelle and us.

Cinderella

From rags to royal, Cinderella’s kindness and dreams brought her to the palace.

Belle

A reader, a dreamer, and a hero. Belle sees beauty within every soul.

Rapunzel

With magical hair and a golden heart, she lights up the world with courage and curiosity.

Tinker Bell

Sassy, sparkly, and smart! This tiny fairy proves that even small wings can fly high.

Moana

Fearless voyager and ocean’s chosen one. Moana follows her heart to save her people and the world.
